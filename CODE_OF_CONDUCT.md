# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not on people
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by contacting the maintainer directly via a private message on GitHub
(@Lorakszak). All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with these standards,
and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behaviour they deem inappropriate.
